Meet some of our maximum glories.
Ariel "El Burrito" Ortega
Player: 1991-96, 2000-02, 2006-08, 2009-10. 361 matches played, 80 goals, 108 assists. 7 titles won.
Marcelo "El Muñeco" Gallardo
Player: 1992-99, 2003-06, 2009-10. 305 matches played, 70 goals. 8 titles won as player.
Coach: 2014-22. 424 matches coached, 228 wins, 111 draws, 85 lost 14 titles won as coach.
Enzo "El Principe" Francescoli
Player: 1983-86, 1994-97. 236 matches played, 136 goals. 7 titles won.
Angel "El Feo" Labruna
Player: 1939-59, 545 matches played, 317 goals. 16 titles won as player.
Coach: 1963, 1968-70, 1975-81. 6 titles won as coach.
